Climate and Soil Requirement for Dargon Fruits

Dragon fruit plant prefers a dry tropical climate with an average temperature of 20-29ºC, but can withstand temperatures of 38-40ºC and as low as 0ºC for short periods. The rainfall requirement of dragon fruit is 1145- 2540 mm/year.

Water Supply in Dargon Fruits

Dragon fruit require anywhere from 25 to50 inches of water per year, and too much irrigation must be cautioned to avoid flower drop and fruit rot. Dragon fruit plants are considered a climbing cactus, and commercial orchards need to use some form of trellising to support the plant.
